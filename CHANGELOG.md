# Changelog

## 0.1.0 (2026-04-05)

Initial release.

- `InstantType` — maps `Instant` to ``TIMESTAMP WITH TIME ZONE``
- `PlainDateTimeType` — maps `PlainDateTime` to ``TIMESTAMP``
- `DateType` — maps `Date` to ``DATE``
- `TimeType` — maps `Time` to ``TIME``
- `type_annotation_map` — ready-to-use dict for SQLAlchemy 2.x `Mapped[]` annotations
